Pranav Vempati

President

Pranav is a recently graduated Computer Science major and a machine learning enthusiast who has lectured at SCAI since 2018. He has interned in various capacities requiring competence in NLP and Computer Vision in the past and conducts research at the Computer Vision Lab at UCSC, designing an application intended to help visually impaired users understand their surroundings. He is proficient in TensorFlow and PyTorch and is additionally interested in systems programming, particularly in modern C++.

Peter Braun

Head Lecturer

Peter Braun is pursuing a PhD at UC Santa Cruz advised by Professor Heiner Litz. His interests lie at the intersection of computer architecture and warehouse-scale compute, with current work on the microarchitecture frontend. He is also interested in the application of machine learning to processor microarchitecture and systems, with projects including data prefetching with deep learning, and identifying reasons for cluster performance degradation with Bayesian causal models.

Rahul Nadkarni

Director of Internal Affairs

Rahul is a second year Computer Engineering major and has been a part of SCAI since 2021. He is new to the AI/ML field and is interested in understanding the multiple facets of this field. He has lead multiple app development projects in Augmented and Virtual Reality development and has published apps to the App Store. He is learning how to use PyTorch and TensorFlow and is interested in using them to solve complex problems.

Webmaster

John Beresford is a multi-disciplinary designer and developer that feels most at home in the intersection of computer graphics and web design/development. He currently writes and runs the lab sections for CSE160 - Computer Graphics at UCSC, and is the webmaster for SCAI.
